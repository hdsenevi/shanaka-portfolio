Who is Behind All This

Hi, I'm Sha Senevirathne

I have over 13 years industry experience and currently work as a Technical Lead / Senior Software Engineer at National Australia Bank. I specializing in native iOS and react-native mobile application development.

I'm a graduate of Sheffield Hallam University MSc Games Software Development with a background in developing games for mobile platforms and serious games for sport, health and wellbeing sectors. I was awarded Ideas4Innovation New Researchers Award 2009, and worked with UK Sport and British Swimming to develop training tools for British swimmers for the London Olympics 2012.

I'm passionate about mobile application development, gaming, game design and real-time application development in general.
