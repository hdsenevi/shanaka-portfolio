Introduction

Your account anywhere, with the MLC app. Making it easier to keep track of your MasterKey accounts.

What you can do on the app

  • Get secure access to your account, using Fingerprint or Face ID on supported devices or a 4-digit PIN.
  • View your super, pension or investment balances - quickly and simply.
  • Check transactions, nominated beneficiaries, your current mix of investment options, and more in your MasterKey accounts.
  • View your personal details and get easy access to help and support within the app.
